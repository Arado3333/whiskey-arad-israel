Golani Distillery was established in 2014 by David Zivell, an immigrant from Canada who settled in Katzrin and decided to turn his hobby, whisky making, into a profession.

The first whisky was distilled in September 2014 and aged in barrels that were previously used in wineries in the Golan Heights. Later on, Zivell raised funds through crowdfunding by selling future whisky bottles and unaged whisky bottles.

Later on, the distillery acquired two pot stills, and the whisky is aged in barrels made from different types of wood, including new American oak.

The distillery produces single malt whisky, liqueurs in flavors of honey and maple, brandy, gin, absinthe, and arak.

The distillery's products are kosher.

The Best Whisky

Golani Distillery Whether it's your first time to the Golan Heights, you live nearby, or visit Israel often, we welcome you with open arms. At Golani Distillery, you will see firsthand how we craft Whisky, Arak & Gin.

Luxury Craftsmanship

Golani Distillery creates exceptional whiskies in the stunning Golan Heights of Israel. Using a carefully curated blend of malted barley and wheat, our spirits are aged for a minimum of 3 years in red wine and new oak barrels, resulting in a perfectly balanced flavor profile.

Award-Winning Excellence

Our commitment to quality has earned Golani Distillery the prestigious Terravino Silver Award in 2016. This prestigious accolade recognizes our whisky as one of the top spirits in its class, celebrated for its outstanding complexity and craftsmanship.

Local Provenance, Global Reach

At the heart of our process is a deep respect for the local environment. Golani sources its ingredients from neighboring farms, ensuring that each bottle is a true reflection of the Golan Heights terroir. Our dedication to sustainability and authenticity allows us to share the unique flavors of Israel with the world.
